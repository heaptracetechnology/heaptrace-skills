What It Is

Knowledge Graphs Make AI Reason, Not Just Match

Vector search finds similar — graphs find related. The two are not interchangeable. Embeddings capture semantic proximity, but they collapse the structure of who-did-what-to-whom into a single dense vector, losing the relationships that make enterprise reasoning possible.

For multi-hop reasoning, regulated audit trails, and entity-centric search, you need a graph layer over your data. When a question requires walking from contract → clause → obligation → counterparty → jurisdiction, vectors will not get you there. Graphs will.

We design domain ontologies, build extraction pipelines, and ship hybrid graph+vector retrieval. Our delivery teams have indexed millions of entities across legal, healthcare, financial services, and enterprise SaaS — with confidence-scored linking and continuous schema evolution baked in.
