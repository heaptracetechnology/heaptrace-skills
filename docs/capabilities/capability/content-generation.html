What It Is

Content Generation That Survives Brand Review

“ChatGPT for marketing” produces output that sounds generic and gets rejected by brand teams. The velocity gain looks great in a demo — until the first round of review brings everything to a halt and the marketing team is back to writing from scratch.

Production content generation requires brand voice modeling, factual guardrails, regulatory awareness, and multi-channel adaptation. Each channel has its own constraints. Each market has its own compliance rules. Each brand has a voice that took years to build — and one bad campaign to lose.

We ship systems where 80%+ of output passes brand review on first pass — and the rest is fixed by tuning, not by writing from scratch. The result is throughput your content ops team feels in week one and a brand team that stops blocking the pipeline.

How We Engineer It

Five Pillars of Production Content Generation

Each pillar is a real engineering practice we've shipped — not a list of features. Skip any one and the system fails brand review in production.

PILLAR 01
Brand Voice Training
Capture voice from existing assets — landing pages, prior campaigns, brand guidelines, executive interviews. Style profile encoded into prompts and constraints. Voice drift monitoring catches deviation before it ships, not after the campaign goes live.
Style Profiling Voice Modeling Drift Detection
PILLAR 02
Variant Generation at Scale
A/B variants, channel adaptations, audience segments. Generate 50 variants in the time a human writes one, with diversity controls to prevent near-duplicate outputs and segment-aware framing for each audience cut.
A/B Variants Segments Diversity
PILLAR 03
Review Workflow Built-In
Queue, approve, reject, comment. Review fatigue is the silent killer of AI content programs — we engineer for it with bulk-approve flows, scoring rubrics, redline diffing, and reviewer load balancing so brand teams stay engaged past week three.
Approval Queue Bulk Review Audit Trail
PILLAR 04
Multi-Channel Output
Email, ad, social, SEO, sales — each with channel-specific constraints, formatting, and CTA patterns. A 90-character meta description, a 1,200-word product page, and a 3-line cold email all come from the same brief without re-prompting from scratch.
Email Ads SEO Sales
PILLAR 05
Performance Feedback Loop
Click-throughs, conversions, engagement feed back into the prompt library. Content that works gets reinforced; patterns that underperform are pruned. The system gets sharper every campaign instead of drifting toward generic output.
Conversion Data Reinforcement Prompt Tuning

Tech Stack

What We Build It With

Pragmatic stack choices — we use the best tool for the job and integrate with what you already run.

01

LLMs

Claude (Sonnet for volume, Opus for hero copy), GPT-4, brand-tuned variants. Model selection per use case — volume runs on cost-efficient tiers, hero campaigns and regulated copy escalate to top-tier models with stricter guardrails.

02

Prompt Management

PromptLayer plus custom prompt versioning. Every prompt change is reviewable, testable, and rollback-safe — we treat prompts like backend code, not config drift.

03

Brand Voice

Custom embedding-based style profiling. Voice fingerprints from existing assets, drift detection on every output, and per-channel style adaptation built into the prompt envelope.

04

Workflow

Sanity, Contentful, custom review apps. We integrate with the CMS your team already uses or build a focused review UI when the existing tooling can't keep up with AI throughput.

05

Channels

Mailgun, SendGrid, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Meta & Google Ads APIs. Approved content flows directly to the channel of record — no copy-paste step that strips formatting or loses tracking.

06

A/B & Performance

Optimizely plus custom feedback ingestion. Click-through, conversion, and engagement signals close the loop into the prompt library so the system reinforces what's actually working in market.

07

Localization

Combined with our Localization capability for review-grade multilingual output. See /capability/localization.html for the full localization stack.

08

Compliance

Regulated-content guardrails and a fact-checking layer for finance, healthcare, and pharma copy. Claim libraries, prohibited-phrase filters, and per-region disclosure injection — all auditable.
