What We Mean

Document Intelligence Is Extraction That Holds Up to Audit

Raw LLM document Q&A is fine for demos. Drop a PDF into a chat window, ask a question, get an answer that sounds confident. Beautiful. But it breaks the moment you put it in front of enterprise scale, accuracy SLAs, or a regulator who wants to know why the model said what it said.

Production document intelligence is a different discipline. It requires structured outputs that downstream systems can consume, confidence scoring on every field, source citation back to the page and paragraph, and PII handling that survives a security review. It requires knowing when the model is guessing — and routing those cases to a human.

We ship pipelines that pass legal and compliance review, not just impressive demos. Every extraction is traceable. Every answer is cited. Every field has a confidence score. That is the difference between a document Q&A toy and a system you can put in front of a claims adjuster, a contract reviewer, or a regulator.

Our Approach

Five Pillars of Production Document Intelligence

Each pillar reflects a place where naive document AI breaks under enterprise scale — and what we engineer instead.

01

Multi-Format Ingestion

PDF, DOCX, HTML, scanned images, emails — the messy reality of enterprise document flow. OCR pipeline tuned per format. Layout-aware parsing for forms, tables, and multi-column documents. No "please convert to plain text first" caveats.

02

Structured Extraction

Output schemas defined upfront with the client — not invented at runtime. Validation rules enforce types, ranges, and cross-field constraints. No free-text fields where structured data is expected. Output goes straight into your database, your CRM, your claims system.

03

Hybrid RAG

Vector search for semantic recall, keyword search for precision, metadata filtering for scope. Source-cited answers down to the page and paragraph. No "the document says…" without a reference a reviewer can click to verify in two seconds.

04

Confidence Scoring

Every field gets a confidence score. Threshold-based routing sends low-confidence extractions to human review, high-confidence ones straight to your downstream systems. No silent low-confidence answers slipping through. The model knows when it doesn't know.

05

PII & Compliance Handling

Redaction at ingest, regional compliance (HIPAA, GDPR, regional data residency), and audit trails on every extraction. Every prompt, every response, every confidence score logged for review. Built to survive a compliance review, not just pass a demo.
