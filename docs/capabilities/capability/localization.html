What It Is

Localization Is More Than Translation

Translation gets the words right; localization gets the market right — currencies, formats, cultural references, regulatory requirements.

1

Translation vs Localization

Translation gets the words right; localization gets the market right — currencies, formats, cultural references, regulatory requirements. The same product needs different copy, different examples, different imagery, and sometimes different features per market.

2

The MT Trade-Off

AI machine translation is fast and cheap, but unreviewed MT in regulated industries is a lawsuit waiting to happen. A mistranslated dosage instruction, a wrong financial disclaimer, a missing GDPR clause — the cost dwarfs any savings.

3

Hybrid Pipelines, Engineered

We engineer hybrid pipelines: AI for speed, human-in-loop for risk, glossaries for consistency, and TMS integration so it lands in your existing workflow. You get throughput without giving up control over what ships.

Tech Stack

Production Localization Stack

Best-of-breed components, integrated. We're tool-agnostic and build to your existing investments.

M

Models

Claude / GPT-4 for cultural adaptation and high-fidelity rewriting. AWS Translate for high-volume baseline MT. MarianMT for on-prem and air-gapped deployments where data residency is required.

T

TMS Integration

Lokalise, Phrase, Smartling, Crowdin — native API integrations for project sync, segment routing, status callbacks, and reviewer assignment.

G

Glossary & TM

SDL Trados-compatible TM stores. Custom term databases with hierarchy, domain tags, and brand-voice metadata. Continuous TM enrichment from approved translations.

Q

QE Models

COMET-based quality estimation as a baseline. Custom per-domain quality estimators trained on your prior approved translations to route only the risky segments to humans.

W

Workflow

Custom reviewer UIs with diff view, glossary inline, and approval queues. Slack and Teams notifications for handoffs, blockers, and SLA breaches. Audit logs for regulated workflows.

i

i18n Infrastructure

ICU MessageFormat for dynamic strings. IETF BCP 47 locale tags. Locale-aware formatting components for date, number, currency, plural, and unit display across web and mobile.

C

Compliance

Per-region content filtering, regulatory glossaries enforced at MT-time, disclaimer injection per market, and segment-level provenance tracking for regulator-ready audit trails.

P

Performance

Caching layer for repeat strings, async batch translation for high-throughput releases, and parallel locale fan-out so 30 languages don't take 30 times longer than English.
