Who We Are

Production Engineers Who Happen to Build AI

Most "AI consultancies" are a few researchers with a handful of contractors. Heaptrace is the opposite — a 200+ person engineering team that has shipped real production software for over a decade, and now ships agentic AI with the same engineering discipline.

Our composition reflects what production AI actually requires: AI engineers, but also QA automation specialists, cloud engineers, data engineers, compliance experts, and domain specialists per industry. The work is multi-disciplinary; so is the team.

We hire for production reliability, not research novelty. We retain by deploying engineers on long-running engagements where they own outcomes — not staff augmentation, not project-and-run.

Team Composition

8 Functional Teams. One Delivery Organization.

A 200+ engineer firm organized into specialized functions — matched to client engagements as composed pods, not a generic bench.

Lead Engineering Team ~15 SENIOR+ ENGINEERS
Architects and tech leads who own delivery quality. Set engineering standards across the firm. Members typically 12+ years experience, often ex-FAANG or similar scale companies. Skills: system design, architecture review, technical leadership, mentorship.
AI-First Engineering Team ~70 ENGINEERS
The largest group. Mid-to-senior engineers shipping agentic AI features, document intelligence pipelines, vision systems, knowledge graphs. Skills: LangGraph, Temporal, RAG, fine-tuning, agent orchestration, eval harness design.
AI QA Automation Team ~25 SPECIALISTS
In-house QA team that has shipped test automation across SaaS, fintech, and regulated environments. Skills: Playwright, Cypress, custom AI test generation, self-healing locators, visual regression, eval harness design.
Cloud & DevOps Team ~30 ENGINEERS

Data Engineering Team ~25 ENGINEERS

Compliance & Security Team ~10 SPECIALISTS
Reviews every regulated engagement. HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, GDPR, FedRAMP awareness. Skills: regulatory framework mapping, audit prep, security review, BAA/DPA contracting.
AI Sales / RevOps Automation ~15 ENGINEERS
Internal team using our own AI capabilities to automate our own sales, RevOps, content. Eats our own dog food at scale — the proving ground for client-facing tooling.
Domain Specialists ~10 SENIOR ADVISORS
Healthcare clinicians, fintech compliance experts, edtech instructional designers, manufacturing quality engineers. Brought into engagements where domain expertise is the gating factor.
