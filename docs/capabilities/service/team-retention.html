Why This Model Exists

Most AI Engagements Fail After the Demo. Team Retention Solves That.

Most AI consulting engagements end at production launch. The vendor exits, the system degrades, models drift, agents go stale, and within 6 months the client's team is back to manual work. The "AI initiative" becomes another shelfware story.

Team Retention exists to break that cycle. Heaptrace embeds a dedicated agentic AI team — engineers, QA, compliance, ML — into your organization for an extended engagement. We don't deliver and leave. We deliver, monitor, improve, and ship the next thing.

The math is simple: a system that's owned and improved monthly outperforms one launched and abandoned. Continuous engagement is how production AI actually works.

Vs Project-Based

Why Long-Term Beats Sprint-Based for AI

AI systems aren't websites. They drift, change behavior, and need owners. Here's what changes when you swap sprint-based for embedded.

Project-Based

  • × Vendor leaves at launch — no one accountable for production behavior.
  • × Model drift goes unmonitored — quality degrades silently.
  • × No ongoing improvement — your AI capability stops at the launch date.
  • × Knowledge walks out the door — next change requires re-learning the system.
  • × Re-contracting friction — every new feature is a new SOW and procurement cycle.

Team Retention

  • Continuous monitoring + improvement — the team that built it owns its production behavior.
  • Retained domain knowledge — institutional memory stays inside the engagement.
  • Evolving roadmap — new capabilities ship in your normal release cadence, not as new projects.
  • Accountable team — one architect, one team, one number to call.
  • Quarterly capability refresh — new models, skills, and domain coverage built in.
