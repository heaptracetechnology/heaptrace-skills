Industry Context

Retail Has Always Been a Content Problem at Scale

Modern retail is a content factory: 800K SKUs need product descriptions, 30 markets need ad copy, 12 channels demand different formats, and brand teams reject anything that sounds generic. The volume is impossible to ship manually; the quality is impossible to ship without humans.

Add the regulatory layer — FTC truth-in-advertising, regional ad standards, age-restricted product handling, GDPR for EU customers, accessibility for inclusive marketing — and "use ChatGPT for marketing" produces output that gets rejected by brand teams or flagged by legal.

We ship retail-AI systems with brand-voice training, regulatory guardrails, and review workflows so 80%+ of generated content passes brand review on first pass — and the rest improves with feedback.

Compliance & Standards

Retail and media content lives in a regulatory landscape. We bake the rules into prompt design, generation guardrails, and review workflows — not as an afterthought.

Regional Ad Standards

Brand Guidelines Enforcement

Inclusive imagery, accessible copy, multilingual variants. Alt-text generation, readability scoring, gendered-language review.
