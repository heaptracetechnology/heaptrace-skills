Context

Logistics Runs on Documents. We Automate the Documents.

Every shipment generates a stack: Bill of Lading, customs declaration, commercial invoice, packing list, carrier paperwork, delivery confirmation. Across modes (ocean, air, ground), regions, and carriers, the formats vary, the requirements shift, and humans burn hours per shipment.

Modern logistics runs on TMS, WMS, ERP, carrier APIs, customs systems — disconnected, exception-heavy, deadline-driven. RPA automated the simple bits. Real workflows need agents that reason across systems and handle the exceptions.

We engineer logistics AI that ingests documents, decides exceptions, calls carrier APIs, files customs, and escalates to humans only when needed — with full audit trail for every shipment.

Operational Standards

Compliance & Standards Built In

Logistics is less about regulators and more about cross-jurisdiction operational standards. We engineer them into every pipeline.

01

Customs Compliance Per Region

US (CBP), EU, China, Mexico — schema and timing requirements baked into the filing layer with region-specific validation before submission.

02

Carrier API Standards

EDI 204/214/990, REST API patterns across major carriers. Adapter layer handles auth, rate limits, retries, and format translation per carrier.

03

Hazmat & Dangerous Goods

IATA DGR, IMDG awareness in classification. Flags hazmat shipments early, blocks bad classifications, and routes mandatory paperwork correctly.

04

Audit-Grade Traceability

Every shipment, every decision, every API call logged. Reproducible runs. Inspectable by ops, compliance, and customs auditors from day one.
