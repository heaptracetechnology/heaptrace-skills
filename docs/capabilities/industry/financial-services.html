Industry Context

AI in Finance Has a Compliance Tax. We've Already Paid It.

Every financial-services AI deployment crosses SR 11-7 (model risk management), KYC/AML obligations, PCI-DSS for any card data, SOX for public-company disclosures, and GDPR for EU customers. Each has audit teeth.

The vendors who say “AI for finance” usually mean a chatbot. Production financial AI requires explainability, lineage, model documentation, and an audit trail every regulator will scrutinize — not a slide deck claiming the model is “explainable” while the team scrambles for evidence the week before validation.

Our finance engagements ship with model documentation, lineage, and audit pipelines as line-item deliverables — not afterthoughts. When MRM, internal audit, or the regulator asks how a decision was made, the answer is one query away.
