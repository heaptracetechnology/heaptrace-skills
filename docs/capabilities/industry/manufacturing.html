The Reality

Manufacturing AI Lives at the Edge — Where Latency and Reliability Are Engineered, Not Marketed

A defect missed on a production line is a defect that ships — sometimes to a customer, sometimes to a recall. Manufacturing AI doesn't get to fail gracefully; it has to detect, classify, and act in real time.

Add OT/IT separation, brownfield environments, vendor-locked PLCs and SCADA systems, supplier onboarding paperwork, ISO 9001 quality requirements — and most "AI for manufacturing" decks fall apart when they meet the shop floor.

Our manufacturing engagements deploy on edge hardware, integrate with existing OT systems, and ship vision and agentic systems where reliability is engineered from inference up.
