Business Pack

Product management — PRDs, user stories, competitor analysis, feature prioritization, proposals, metrics, and stakeholder updates.

8 Skills
prd-write
Write a complete Product Requirements Document (PRD) from an idea, feature request, or business need. Produces a structured, stakeholder-ready document covering problem statement and success metrics.
business / prd-write
user-story
Write well-structured user stories with persona identification, goal mapping, Given/When/Then acceptance criteria, edge cases, dependencies, and story splitting techniques.
business / user-story
competitor-analysis
Analyze competitor products and features systematically. Produces feature matrices, SWOT analysis per competitor, gap analysis, pricing comparisons, and market positioning maps.
business / competitor-analysis
feature-prioritize
Prioritize features and backlog items using proven frameworks — RICE scoring, MoSCoW method, impact/effort matrix, and weighted scoring. Turns a messy list into a data-driven roadmap.
business / feature-prioritize
client-proposal
Write a professional feature proposal for a client engagement. Produces executive summary, problem analysis, proposed solution, technical approach, phased timeline, and ROI projection.
business / client-proposal
metrics-define
Define KPIs and success metrics for a feature or product. Covers what to measure, how to instrument, dashboard layout, success thresholds, leading vs lagging indicators, and A/B test design.
business / metrics-define
scope-negotiate
Negotiate and manage project scope with stakeholders and clients. Produces must-have vs nice-to-have analysis, MVP definition, phased delivery plans, and trade-off matrices.
business / scope-negotiate
stakeholder-update
Write professional status updates for stakeholders, clients, and leadership. Produces progress summaries, metric highlights, blocker analysis, decisions needed, and risk register updates.
business / stakeholder-update
